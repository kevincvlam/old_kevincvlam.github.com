Hello World.

Kevin Lam is an Engineering student at the University of Toronto. He recently completed his third-year of studies in the Electrical and Computer Option of Engineering Science, and is currently working as an interning at Analog Devices Inc. as a member of the High Speed ADC group.

Kevin was born and raised in Mississauga. He is chinese-vietnamese by blood, but Canadian by home. Growing up he has always had a passion for the written and visual arts; and in his high school years he grew to love writing and performing music as well.
