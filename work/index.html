Design

Scribble.

Kevin has accumulated over 6 years of experience in the fine arts, having focused on the use of traditional mediums: pencils, pastels, oils, acrylics, poster paints, water colours, and charcoals. These skills can been seen in Kevin's approach to design.

Photography

Snap.

Kevin is only recently starting to learn more about photography, but has always enjoyed taking pictures. He uses a Panasonix Lumix DMC-GF1, documenting all kinds of things including adventures around Toronto, as well as adventures in the kitchen!

Sound

Squeak.

Kevin started playing piano at a young age. Over the years he added guitar, trumpet and drums to the mix but these days focuses on his guitar-playing.

Kevin has recently performed with the Skule Jazz Combo, the Skule Nite Orchestra, the Skule Stage Band, and a Top 40 Cover band. He loves music of all genres, with a particular love of jazz.
